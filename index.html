About

Full Professor and Chair of Computer Architecture & Parallel Systems at the Technical University of Munich (since 2017); member of the Board of Directors at LRZ. Previous roles include LLNL/CASC and Cornell University. Ph.D. (TUM, 2001), M.S. (UIUC). Chair of the MPI Forum; 250+ peer‑reviewed publications.

Contact: schulzm@in.tum.de · Garching campus · More at CAPS profile.
